# Human Handoff

Do these steps manually after reviewing the draft.

1. Record the voiceover from `voiceover_script.md`.
2. Optional: record short clips for:
   - Codex provider self-test.
   - GoalChainer JSON output.
   - PeTTaChainer proof-audit output.
3. Put the recordings in `public/recordings/` using the filenames listed there.
4. Render the final MP4 with `npm run render:clean`.
5. Upload the final MP4 to your YouTube channel as unlisted.
6. Paste the YouTube URL into the Deep Projects pitch video field.
7. Click `Update Deliverables` only after you have reviewed the URL and repo
   links.

Do not submit the current placeholder draft as the final public video.
